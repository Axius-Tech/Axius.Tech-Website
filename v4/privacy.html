Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Axius operates an independent technology operations practice. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, contact us, or engage Axius for a paid engagement, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

  • Contact information — name and email when you submit a contact form, book a discovery call, or email us directly.
  • Diagnostic answers — the industry / friction / impact answers you provide in our on-site diagnostic. These live only in your browser (localStorage, key axius:perso:v1) and are never sent to Axius unless you explicitly submit the contact form.
  • Usage analytics — basic, aggregate analytics from our hosting platform (Vercel) such as page views and referrers. No tracking cookies are set.
  • Conversation records — if you reach out, we keep your message and our replies in our email or internal messaging tools so we can serve you.

How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and serve clients we have engaged.
  • To improve the service and the website.
  • To send transactional information about a paid engagement (invoices, reports).

We do not send marketing emails without your prior consent.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with a small set of vetted service providers required to run Axius:

  • Stripe — payment processing for monthly subscriptions.
  • Cal.com — booking and calendar scheduling.
  • Telegram — Axius's internal operator notifications.
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure.

Each of these processors has their own privacy policies and security practices that govern their handling of any data we pass to them.

Cookies and local storage

The Axius website does not set tracking cookies. We use localStorage for one purpose: to remember the answers you give in our on-site diagnostic so the website can show you relevant capabilities and recommendations on subsequent visits. The key is axius:perso:v1 and it expires automatically after 30 days. You can clear it at any time from your browser settings.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold by writing to andres@axius.tech. We respond within 30 days.

Children

Axius's services are intended for businesses and their authorized representatives. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

Changes to this policy

We will post material changes here with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be communicated to active clients via email.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Email andres@axius.tech.
